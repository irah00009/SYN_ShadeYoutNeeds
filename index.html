About Us

SYN (Shade Your Needs)

SYN (Shade Your Needs) is all about making eyewear shopping simple and stress-free. SYN allows users to browse a wide selection of frames while using the virtual try on feature to see how each pair looks on you in real time. It is designed to help customers find the glasses that actually fit their style and personality without having to leave their homes. Everything is designed to be easy, comfy, and personal so you can explore, try on, and choose the perfect pair with confidence.
